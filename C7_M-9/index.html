Exponents and Powers

See The Chapter PDF

Short Answer Questions(SAQs)

What are ‘Exponents’?

An exponent is a number that is placed as a superscript over a number. In other words, it indicates that the base is raised to a certain power.

What is an ‘Exponent zero’?

The zero exponent indicates that there are no factors of a number.

What is a ‘Standard form’?

Standard form is a way of writing a number so it is easier to read. It is often used for very large or very small numbers.

MCQs

1. What is 23?

(a) 5

(b) 6

(c) 8

(d) 9

► (c) 23 = 2 × 2 × 2 = 8.

2. What is 100?

(a) 10

(b) 1

(c) 0

(d) None of these

► (b) Any non-zero number to the power 0 is 1.

3. What is 51?

(a) 0

(b) 5

(c) 1

(d) 10

► (b) 51 = 5.

4. What is the value of 32?

(a) 6

(b) 8

(c) 9

(d) 12

► (c) 32 = 3 × 3 = 9.

5. Which of the following is written in standard form?

(a) 7,000

(b) 7 × 103

(c) 70

(d) 700

► (b) 7 × 103 is written in standard exponential form.

6. What is the base in 42?

(a) 4

(b) 2

(c) 8

(d) 16

► (a) 4 is the base, 2 is the exponent.

7. What is the exponent in 64?

(a) 6

(b) 24

(c) 4

(d) 10

► (c) 4 is the exponent (or power).

8. 24 equals:

(a) 8

(b) 12

(c) 16

(d) 24

► (c) 2 × 2 × 2 × 2 = 16.

9. 1n is always:

(a) n

(b) 0

(c) 1

(d) None of these

► (c) Any power of 1 is always 1.

10. What is 90?

(a) 9

(b) 1

(c) 0

(d) 90

► (b) 90 = 1 by zero exponent rule.

11. Which of the following has the greatest value?

(a) 25

(b) 33

(c) 42

(d) 51

► (a) 25 = 32; 33 = 27; 42 = 16; 51 = 5; so (a) is greatest.

12. 102 is equal to:

(a) 20

(b) 100

(c) 1000

(d) 10

► (b) 10 × 10 = 100.

13. 31 + 30 equals:

(a) 4

(b) 3

(c) 1

(d) 2

► (a) 3 + 1 = 4.

14. The exponent in 72 tells us:

(a) How many times 7 is multiplied by itself

(b) Number of zeros

(c) Number of base digits

(d) None of these

► (a) 7 × 7 = 49. So exponent shows how many times base is used as a factor.

15. What is the result of 03?

(a) 0

(b) 3

(c) 1

(d) Undefined

► (a) 0 multiplied 3 times is still 0.

16. What is the expanded form of 53?

(a) 5 × 3

(b) 5 + 5 + 5

(c) 5 × 5 × 5

(d) 15

► (c) Exponents represent repeated multiplication.

17. Which is the correct statement?

(a) 23 = 32

(b) 24 = 16

(c) 42 = 8

(d) 101 = 0

► (b) 24 = 16 is the only correct equation.

18. 60 equals:

(a) 0

(b) 1

(c) 6

(d) Undefined

► (b) Any non-zero base raised to 0 is 1.
